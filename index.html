Changes We've Implemented

This year marks a pivotal shift in how our department contributes to the overall operations. We've gone beyond surface-level case handling and began analyzing complaints in-depth to identify systemic issues and propose improvements. Many of these suggestions are now being implemented company-wide.

  • Operational Improvements

    We now provide structured improvement suggestions based on case insights — and operations is actively implementing them.

  • VIP Guardians

    A specialized focus on high-priority customers with tailored handling strategies.

  • Influencer Partnerships

    We've launched our first influencer partnership and are working on expanding this initiative. Such as but not limited to our partnership with Reddit and our Internal Complaints

  • Departamental Changes

    Agent headcount grew from 8 in 2024 to 13 by July 2025 — a 62.5% increase in staffing.
